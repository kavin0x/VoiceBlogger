# Privacy Policy for VoiceBlogger

**Last updated: June 5, 2026**

## Overview

VoiceBlogger is designed with privacy as a core principle. All AI processing — speech recognition and blog generation — happens entirely on your device. Your voice recordings and generated content never leave your iPhone.

## Data We Collect

**We collect no personal data.** VoiceBlogger has no accounts, no servers, and no analytics.

## What Stays on Your Device

The following data is created and stored locally on your device only:

- **Voice recordings** — audio files captured through your microphone
- **Transcriptions** — text generated from your recordings by the on-device Whisper model
- **Blog posts** — content generated by the on-device Gemma language model
- **App preferences** — settings stored in iOS UserDefaults (e.g., whether models have been downloaded)

None of this data is transmitted to us or any third party.

## AI Models

On first launch, VoiceBlogger downloads two AI model files from [Hugging Face](https://huggingface.co):

- **openai/whisper-medium** — for speech-to-text transcription
- **mlx-community/gemma-4-e2b-it-4bit** — for blog and caption generation

These downloads are the only outbound network requests the app makes after the initial setup. Once downloaded, the models are stored on your device and all inference runs locally. Hugging Face's own [privacy policy](https://huggingface.co/privacy) governs those download requests.

## Permissions

| Permission | Why It's Needed |
|---|---|
| **Microphone** | To record your voice for transcription |

No other device permissions are requested or used.

## Data Sharing

We do not sell, share, or transmit your data to any third party — because we never receive it in the first place.

## Data Retention and Deletion

All recordings and blog posts are stored in your app's local sandbox. You can delete them at any time from within the app. Uninstalling VoiceBlogger removes all associated data from your device.

## Children's Privacy

VoiceBlogger does not knowingly collect information from anyone, including children under 13. Since no data is collected at all, no special handling is required.

## Changes to This Policy

If we update this policy, we will revise the "Last updated" date above and note the change in the app's release notes.
